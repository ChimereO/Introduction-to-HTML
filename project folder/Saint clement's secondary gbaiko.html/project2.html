saint clement secondary school,gbaiko

st clement secondary school is located in gbaiko, minna, niger state.
it was founded in the year2005.
st clement has the total number of students of approximately 700 students.
st clement is registered to some of the major educational boards in NIgeria which are: the Joint Admission Matriculation Board(JAMB),the west African Examination Council(WAEC), and the National Examination Eouncil(NECO).
